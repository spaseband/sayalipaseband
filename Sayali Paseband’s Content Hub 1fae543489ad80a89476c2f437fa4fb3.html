Hi, I’m Sayali Paseband — a cybersecurity expert with over a decade of experience in securing the digital world. Currently, I serve as an Advisor, Cybersecurity Engineering at Verisk, and previously contributed to global cloud security initiatives at Amazon Web Services.

With 7+ academic degrees in cybersecurity and IT — including a Master’s in Cybersecurity Analytics, an MBA in IT Management, and a Cybersecurity Professional Certificate from Harvard University — I’ve dedicated my career to making complex security concepts accessible and actionable.

I’m also a published author, YouTuber, and lifelong learner who loves sharing insights on cybersecurity, cloud, and digital resilience.
